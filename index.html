Welcome to Wellfield

Welcome to the Wellfield School website. We are proud of our traditions and our commitment to the future. Our school is a place of learning, discovery, and growth. We invite you to explore our community and see what makes Wellfield a special place for your child's education.

About Our School

"Wellfield School is based in Leyland, supporting kids aged 12-17. We have an amazing, dedicated staff team. We strive to go the extra mile, and follow our values which hold our school together."

At the Heart of Wellfield

At the heart of Wellfield, we follow our values: Clarity, Consistency and Communication. We believe in nurturing all students into the true people they are. We take education to the next level - focusing on realism, accuracy and professionalism.

Clarity

Clear goals and transparent expectations for every student.

Consistency

A steady and reliable environment to foster growth.

Communication

Strong partnerships between staff, students, and parents.

We invite you to explore our campus and learn more about the dynamic and nurturing environment we provide. Our work aims to stabilise and strengthen our community, making Wellfield School an exciting centre for learning for our partnerships and beyond.
